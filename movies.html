Movies I recommend

Loving Vincent
Dorota Kobiela, Hugh Welchman
A beautiful movie about the life of Vincent van Gogh. Told through his paintings. The movie's animation are handpainted in oil on approximately 65.000 canvasses.
Stolz & Vorurteil
Joe Wright
A romantic view in the past and their communication style.
Persepolis
Marjane Satrapi, Vincent Paronnaud
An animated black and white movie about a graphic novelist's self-portrait and her life in Iran and Europe in the 1970's.
Match Point
Woody Allen
A quiet but also dramatic thriller with smartly played symbolism of luck, time and destiny.
Midnight in Paris
Woody Allen
A movie about dreamers that glorify the past and how they find romanticism even in the moment.
Alois Nebel
Tomás Lunák
Also a black and white animation about a man living in the Czech Republic, close to the polish border, in the late 1980's.
Das Parfüm
Tom Tykwer
A thriller/drama about the sense, fewer people pay attention to - the smell, and a boy that was born with the rare distinction of a great smelling sense. It gives an insight in people's subconscious, the play of seduction and greed.
Nightmare Before Christmas
Henry Selick
A wonderful stop-motion movie that teaches tolerance of differences.
Das letzte Einhorn
Arthur Rankin, Jules Bass
A beautiful animation from the 1980's that plays with human emotions, weaknesses but also strengths in a setting of characters from old mythologies and legends.
Sweeney Todd
Tim Burton
A musical about a dramatic destinity of a barber in the industrial London.
